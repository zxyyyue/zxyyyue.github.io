## Why This Topic Matters

Delayed feedback is a core challenge in online advertising and recommendation systems, especially when conversion signals arrive much later than clicks or other shallow actions.

## Key Questions

- How can we distinguish true negatives from yet-unobserved positives?
- How should post-click trajectories be modeled?
- Can generative models help recover future evidence under partial observation?

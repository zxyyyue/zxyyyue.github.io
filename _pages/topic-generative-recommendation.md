## Why This Topic Matters

Generative recommendation aims to move beyond conventional retrieval-and-ranking pipelines and explore unified generation-based paradigms for recommendation, ranking, and user behavior modeling.

## Key Questions

- Can generative models replace or unify parts of the traditional recommendation pipeline?
- How should sequence modeling and candidate generation be integrated?
- What are the trade-offs between generation quality, controllability, and serving cost?
